- Bio -

She's like a possum - the disease she carries doesn't affect her, but instead slowly affects everything around her

She appears in sullen places. Old games, the half-furnished bedrooms of childhood homes, movies left to slowly corrupt on an old VHS tape.

Everything rots around her, the insides slowly corrupting, viscous and red, bleeding and pale, becoming unrecognizable while chunks of wall fall off in gross, chewy slabs.

Thankfully this disease does not affect the living, only the things you used to love.
