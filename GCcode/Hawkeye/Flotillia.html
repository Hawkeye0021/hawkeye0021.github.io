Decree Members Join & Support

The Royal Court of Flotillia has issued a decree!


     My fellow Flotsam, it brings me great sadness to see our ranking at #49 on the Neopet Popularity page. Are we not majestic? Are we truly not as appealing as these Aisha and Lupes? Are we not worthy of the love and adoration of Neopians far and wide? Of course we are! Let us swim our way to a higher place in their hearts! We must take matters into our own hands and spread the glory of Flotsam to all corners of Neopia!

     I, Flotillia the Royal Flotsam, hereby establish the order of the Flotsam Flotilla! It is our mission, nay, our duty to increase our numbers, to inspire the love and adoration you deserve! We shall not stop until every Neopian has let a Flotsam into their heart and their home. Rejoice! For today we rise above our place in Neopia! We strive for something greater, we strive to love and to be loved!


Join us in this mission, do not be a bystander as we make history.

Join the Flotsam Flotilla today!

Yours truly,

  Flotillia
